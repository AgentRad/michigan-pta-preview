Our story

A century of showing up for Michigan kids.

Founded in Battle Creek on May 19, 1918, Michigan PTA is a chapter of the nation's oldest and largest child-advocacy association, born from the National Congress of Mothers in 1897.

Through every era, from child labor law and public health to civil rights, school safety, and mental health, PTA volunteers have stood with families and educators to make children's lives better.

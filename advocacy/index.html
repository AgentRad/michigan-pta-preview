Advocacy that shows up where decisions get made.

Michigan PTA carries the voice of families and educators to Lansing and Capitol Hill. Resolutions, position statements, and the toolkit that turns members into advocates.

2026 Priorities

Where we're pushing this year.

Michigan PTA's legislative priorities are voted on annually by member delegates at the State Convention.

Mental Health Funding

Sustained state funding for school-based mental health staff and student supports across every district.

Special Education Staffing

Closing the gap between IEP requirements and available certified specialists in Michigan classrooms.

Clean School Air

Air quality standards for school buildings, with state-funded HVAC upgrades for older facilities.

Open Meetings & Transparency

Strong School Board open-meetings practices so parents can participate and watchdog effectively.
